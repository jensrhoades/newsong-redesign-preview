What types of remodeling do you offer?
Whole-house renovations, second-story additions, historic restorations, basements, ADUs, kitchens, baths, porches, and exterior work.
How long does a typical remodel take?
Most projects run 6–12 months depending on scope. We manage the schedule closely and keep you updated the whole way.
Do you work on historic homes?
It's what we're best at. Craftsman, Victorian, colonial, mid-century modern, we work on all of them.
Do you pull permits?
Yes. As a licensed Georgia general contractor, we pull all necessary permits with the local municipality or county.
What about warranties?
Every project carries a one-year written warranty on workmanship and products, excluding normal wear and tear.
