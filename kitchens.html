How much does a kitchen remodel cost in Atlanta?
It depends on size, finishes, and whether you're moving walls or plumbing. Our instant pricing tool gives you a realistic range in minutes, and we firm it up at the consult.
Can I use my own cabinets or fixtures?
Often yes. We'll tell you honestly where a selection helps and where it might cause fit or warranty issues.
Will I be able to use my kitchen during the remodel?
For most full remodels there's a stretch without a working kitchen. We plan around it and give you a clear timeline up front.
Do you handle permits and inspections?
Yes. As a licensed Georgia general contractor, we pull all permits and manage inspections with the county or city.
